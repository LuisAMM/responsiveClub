Hiking Club

Meet up. Work out. Reach the Peak

Do you feel like you are always sitting in your chair at home or at work, do you want to get more exercise, then you should join our community of hikers whose goal is to train hard to reach higher peaks.

Hikers Club is a free MeetUp group sponsored by North Face.

Learn about some of the most interesting destinations that we have visited in our club.

Mount Everest is Earth's highest mountain above sea level, with 8848 meters, located in the Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the Himalayas.

The Zugspitze at 2,962 m above sea level, is the highest peak of the Wetterstein Mountains as well as the highest mountain in Germany.

Aconcagua is a mountain in the Principal Cordillera of the Andes mountain range, in Mendoza Province, Argentina. It is the highest mountain in the Americas,with a summit elevation of 6,961 metres.
